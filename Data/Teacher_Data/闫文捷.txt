Prior to joining the College of Media and International Culture she was an Assistant Professor in the Edward R. Murrow College of Communication at Washington State University. Her research interests are centered on the interplay between media effects individuals’ psychology and their communicative behaviors in public deliberation. She is also interested in the ways that the new digital media may help individuals form attitudes express opinions and participate in public life in contemporary China.
