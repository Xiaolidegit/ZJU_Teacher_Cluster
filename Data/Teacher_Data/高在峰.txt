With a user-friendly GUI KBC offers three core functions: recording the BM of humans (Recording) editing the recorded BM (Movie Editing) and playing the recorded BM in the playing window (Movie Playing). Furthermore KBC enables users to freely select their preferred parameters by defining target skeleton points view options number of agents in motion and whether hand gestures are recorded.
