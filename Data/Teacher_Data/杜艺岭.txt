杜艺岭医学院基础医学Microbial Specailized Metabolism Biosynthesis Natural Product Chemistry Microbial Chemical Signalling Synthetic Biology Biocatalysis个人简介:Microbes produce a variety of drug molecules with diversified chemical structures and biological activities. These molecules also known as natural products are essential to human health and have been used to combat infectious bacteria and fungi. Dr. Yiling Dus research integrates microbial genetics molecular biology and biochemistry organic chemistry and structural biology approaches to study natural product biosynthetic machinery. The goal is to understand how these small molecules are made in nature how microbes control their biosynthetic pathways whats the ecological role of these chemical molecules and then engineer microbal chemistry for drug discovery (Nat Chem Biol 2017 Nat Chem Biol 2016 PNAS 2015)Selected publications:Yi-Ling Du Katherine S Ryan (2018) Pyridoxal phosphate-dependent reactions in the biosynthesis of natural products.
