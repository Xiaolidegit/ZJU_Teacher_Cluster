王立刚物理学系物理学· 光场调控、相干光学、传输光学、弯曲时空光学· 量子光学、光与新型材料作用、群速度调控个人简介:王立刚（Li-Gang Wang)，博士，教授，博士生导师。Li-Gang Wang is a professor of Physics at Zhejiang University. He received his Master degree from Zhejiang University in 2002 and got his Ph. D degree from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2005. Then he served as Associate Professor at Department of Physics before 2015. Between 2008 and 2010 he was a Research Assistant Professor at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. He also did his postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at Texas A&M University from 2013 to 2014. He had also visited Center for Quantum Physics at CIIT Pakistan and visited the National Center for Applied Physics at KACST Saudi Arabia. He has published over 90 research papers on topics such as beam/pulse propagations optical coherence effects optical tweezers light propagation in photonic crystals and novel materials (metamaterials graphene etc) slow and fast-light propagations in various systems.主要项目情况:主持完成国家自然科学基金青年、面上基金项目等多项，参与973子课题及国家重点研发计划项目等。研究兴趣包括：1、具Dirac能带结构的2D或3D材料与光场的相互作用；2、光与光子晶体的相互作用及光场时空演化、色散理论、群速度调控、物理光学等；3、激光光镊理论及部分相干光学、以及光与多能级原子相互作用等。研究关键鉰：Goos-Hanchen shift Group velocity (fast-light and slow-light) Dirac point Optical dispersion Partially coherent Optical radiation forces， coherence photonic crystals.  招生信息： 常年欢迎高年级本科生来本课题小组参与科研训练计划，要求：无专业课重修者，认真对待自己决定且能始终。 欢迎有志于学术研究的硕士及博士研究生报考，报考前请事先联系。   Group news:5、 Join Chinese Physics Society 2018 Autumn Conference and one students gave an oral presentation and other two students posted their current works.
