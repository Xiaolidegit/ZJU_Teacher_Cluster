在水下机器人控制技术研究中应用滑模PID混合控制方法及矢量推力分配技术，对我国4500米级深海资源自主勘查潜水器系统可回转推进器的运动控制问题和侧向海流干扰下的航迹精确跟踪性能有重要改善，显著提高了潜水器系统的综合航行控制性能。2.在深水机器人导航研究中提出多导航传感器鲁棒估计、水下自动标定、导航自主性方法，突破了不确定测量数据扰动下的鲁棒估计和单站纯距离自主导航等难题，极大改善了深水器机器人导航精度，为我国唯一6000米无人无缆潜器”潜龙一号”海底科学调查的成功应用发挥了关键作用。3. 提出了基于声信息传输的水面机器人自主跟踪水下机器人的跟踪控制方法，采用轨迹预测、自主行为等方法突破了弱通信环境下的跟踪技术问题，完成了湖上演示试验验证，为多水下机器人水面与水下的协作控制奠定了基础。4.主导完成我国第一台6000米深海机器人长基线声学定位导航系统国产化改造，提出了深海应答器位置的垂线相交标定法，解决了深海环境下未知海底声信标位置的准确估计问题，显著提高了长基线导航定位系统的定位精度。5.主要完成我国第一台长航程水下自动观测系统，设计了由多波束、水流剖面仪、温盐深测量仪、实时声速仪等传感器集成的自主海洋观测和处理系统，取得了大量的高质量水下探测数据。6.协助主持完成我国首型长航程水下机器人系统研制与一级定型，解决了水下机器人故障诊断、应急处理、导航参数标定等问题，领导和完成了设定定型资料的准备工作，为该型号项目顺利通过一级定型奠定了重要基础。 APPLICATIONWelcome aboard!Located  in HangzhouZhejiang ProvinceZhejiang University is one of China`s  leading universities. We are widely recognized for outstanding academic  quality in our education and research.Zhejiang  University Ocean College was officially established in December 2012  to better serve and support State’s ocean strategy and echo local  government’s requirements.  “Integrated Sustainable and Internationalized development” highlight the college`s philosophy.  Ocean College utilizes the favorable policies of New Marine Economic  Zone of Zhoushan and multi-disciplinary resources of Zhejiang University  to establish a unique and comprehensive training system covering the  undergraduate education the postgraduate education and the continuing  education of high-level scholars and to develop into the comprehensive  research-intensive institute with distinguished features in marine  science and ocean engineering.There  are two departments under Ocean College namely the Department of  Marine Science and the Department of Ocean Engineering. OC offers four  undergraduate programs: Ocean engineering and technology Port waterway  and coastal engineering Marine science and Naval architecture and  ocean engineering. OC provides two primary disciplinary master programs  and five secondary disciplinary doctoral program.Research  institutes in Ocean College includes Institute of Marine Geology &  Resources Institute of Physical Oceanography Institute of Marine  Biology & Natural Products Institute of Marine Chemistry and  Environment Institute of Island and Coastal Research Institute of  Port Coastal and Nearshore Engineering Institute of Underwater  Technology and Institute of Naval Architecture & Ocean Engineering.A  new campus exclusively for Ocean College is put into use at the  island city of Zhoushan Zhejiang Province. Zhoushan campus covers an  area of 191636 square meters(56 hectares) including 167624 square  meters above-ground and 24012 square meters underground area. Zhoushan  campus will be equipped with world-class laboratories and  state-of-the-art experiment and facilities.Ocean  College is welcoming talents from home and abroad and looking forward  to making a remarkable progress and a prosperous future together with  you! In  the purpose of further facilitating talents coming to China for their  academic pursuit Chinese government together with the Sate Oceanic  Administration jointly set up the Marine scholarship of China for the  purpose of providing financial support to outstanding students from the  coastal countries or regions around the South China Sea the Pacific and  the Indian Oceans and African developing countries to study in China  for a master’s or doctoral degree in oceanography or other related  specialties cultivating advanced professionals of the related fields  for developing countries enhancing international marine cooperation and  exchange and promoting the harmonious ocean development in the region  and around the world. This  webpage is designed as a fast and easy reference to the  marine scholarship. We would be delighted to provide you with more  information if you contact us at any of the links provided.
