He was appointed at ZJU in 2013 following his position as an Associate Researcher in the Institute of Biomedical Engineering (The University of Oxford). His appointment at ZJU was primarily to lead initiatives surrounding biomaterials and tissue engineering. He obtained his PhD in Biomedical Engineering from University College London (2011) and has served as an R&D manager and principal investigator for five years at the Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI Taiwan). He has published over 40 scientific articles and has been granted 32 international patents in the development of flexible/rigid healthcare devices for biomedical applications which have been directly transferred into several industrial sectors. He serves as a member of the FIT academic committee (Zhejiang University) and also as a member of the Oversea’s High-level Talents of Zhejiang Province China. Engineering are available ! 教学与课程:授课课程(Teaching)：-生物材料与组织工程(Biomaterials and Tissue Engineering)本课程内容主要包括生物材料和组织工程的基础知识和发展动向。通过本课程的教学工作，使学生能够明确生物材料和组织工程的基本内容，认识生物材料研究现状与发展趋势、来源及制备方法，以及在组织工程的应用，从而激发学生在生物材料和组织工程及相关研究的兴趣。生物材料与组织工程的教学目的是向学生介绍生物材料設計、製備與检测技术、研究概况和发展趋势，以及组织工程学的基本原理和研究现状。培养学生具有生物材料和组织工程设计的基本思路，使学生具备从事生物材料和组织工程研究及其相关领域的知识与能力。-生物医用材料技术与应用(Technology and Applications of Biomedical Materials)本课程的教学内容主要介绍各种生物医学材料（金属材料、陶瓷材料、高分子材料、复合材料、微纳米材料与仿生材料）的化学成分、组织结构、成形技术、性能与其临床应用实例。通过本课程的教学工作，学生能够明确掌握生物医学材料的物理和力学方面性质、材料性能的表征方法、材料的生物界面反应、材料的降解和特殊功能设计合成的理论基础、材料结构和功能之间的联系、材料表面改性和生物组织之间的设计方法和原理、不同临床应用的材料选择与实验技能，以及生物医用材料的检测评价方法与临床应用中存在的问题和情况等，从而激发学生在生医材料及相其关研究的兴趣，为学生在生物医学工程相关的专业知识打下坚实的基础。-Biomaterials (ZJU-NUS Programme)This course covers an advanced knowledge of topics in Biomaterials and Tissue Engineering and in associated subjects. The course is designed to be inter-disciplinary and attractive to physical science engineering and life sciences students. The major educational aims are to provide fundamental knowledge in Biomaterials Tissue Engineering and related processing involved with cellular interaction with the materials.
