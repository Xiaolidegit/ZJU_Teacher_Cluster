Control of Network Systems杰出审稿人本科生项目:本科生毕业设计金融体系的影响因素识别和预测Influence Identification and Prediction of Financial Systems项目描述：现在，世界的金融体系变得越来越复杂并高度耦合。要了解不同群体在市场产生的影响或者不同国内市场的影响变得很重要。然而，这种影响很难直接衡量。 在实际过程中，我们通常实时观察股票价格或股票指数。 有时我们也可以观察这些群体或市场之间的实时或延迟的交易行为。 该项目旨在将机器学习中的经典网络层析成像理论和工具应用于国际金融关系，目的是解释隐藏在该关系上数据流的影响架构。Project Description: Today the world’s financial system is becoming more and more dynamic and interconnected. It is important to understand the influence between different parties playing in a common market or influence between various domestic markets. Such influence is however difficult to measure directly. In practice we have observations often in real-time equity prices or stock indices. We sometimes can also observe the real-time or delayed flows of transactions among those parties or markets. The project seeks to apply classical network tomography theory and tools from machine learning to the international financial map with the aim of uncovering the influence structure that is hidden from the data flowing over that map.指导教师：Dr Guodong Shi Australian National University/The University of Sydney                  Dr Junfeng Wu Zhejiang University对手的社交计算Social Computing with Adversaries项目描述：社交网络随着人类决策和机器算法进步而发展，这就有了社交计算的定义。 社交计算系统包括推荐系统，页面排名算法等，并且它也成为社交行为研究的平台。 研究社交计算的性能或鲁棒性的一个关键挑战在于人类行为的不确定性，即对手持续存在。 对手可以生成用来注入社交计算系统的虚假数据，或者可以改变易受干扰的社交成员的决策。 该项目旨在研究这种对抗行为在社交计算中的影响，以及设计保护机制可以增强社交网络上决策制定的鲁棒性。Project Description:Social networks are evolving according to both human decisions and machine algorithms which lead to the definition of social computing. Recommender systems page rank algorithms etc. are all examples and such social computing systems which also become platforms for the study of social behaviors. A key challenge in understanding the performance or robustness of social computing lies in the uncertainty from the human part where adversaries persist. Adversaries can generate fake data injected into the social computing system or manipulate the decisions of vulnerable social members. This project aims to study the influence of that adversarial behaviors in social computing and design protection mechanism which can potentially enhance the robustness of descion-making over social networks.指导教师：Dr Guodong Shi Australian National University/The University of Sydney                  Dr Junfeng Wu Zhejiang University无人机智能避障Smart Collision Avoidance for Unmanned Aerial Vehicles项目描述：学生将会在2-3个机器人中设计并实现防撞解决方案。学生通过比较不同的传感器来进行选择，并用于他们自己的项目中。他们将在检测到障碍物时设计相关避免碰撞算法。该算法要简单、灵敏以及主动预测相关不确定性、其他机器人的踪迹或者通信。学生将通过考虑“能力”（即能够在没有碰撞的空间中安全操作的机器人的数量）来评估其避障解决方案的性能来结束此项目。Project Description:The students will design and implement a collision avoidance solution in a team of 2-3 robots. The students will compare different sensors for collision detection and choose the one for their projects. They will design a collision avoidance algorithm upon a detection of potential collisions. The algorithm can be simple and reactive or more proactive that involves uncertain intention prediction the tracking of other robots or communication. The students will end the project with an evaluation of the performance of their collision avoidance solution by considering “capacity” i.e. the number of robots that can safety operate in a space without collision.指导教师：Prof. Yan Wan University of Texas at Arlington                     Dr Junfeng Wu Zhejiang UniversityReferences:1.
