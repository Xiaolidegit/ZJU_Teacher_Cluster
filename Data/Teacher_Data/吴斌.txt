He obtained his Ph.D. in the field of organic chemistry in 2007 at the Department of Chemistry Zhejiang University (Hangzhou China). Subsequently he was relocated to the Pharmaceutical College Zhejiang University where he pursued research in the field of Medicinal Chemistry as a Post-doc research fellow Assistant Professor and Associate Professor. In 2011 he moved his lab to the Ocean College Zhejiang University  where he was promoted as a full Professor. He is now engaged in a range of projects related to the discovery of new natural products from the sea.
