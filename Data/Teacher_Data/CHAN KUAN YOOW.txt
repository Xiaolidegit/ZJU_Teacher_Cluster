Molecular Biochemical Parasitology. 174(2):137-40.科研:This programme coupled with emerging biomarkers identified from databases such as the Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA) will provide means to identify novel signaling networks involved in cell cycle regulation. By introducing single mutations into untransformed human cells and comparing how unmodified cells and mutated cells respond differently to various cellular stresses like DNA damage or replication stress; I can start examining how individual mutations can contribute to the gross cell cycle deregulation experienced in cancer. Untangling the wealth of genetic information obtained by cancer genome sequencing efforts worldwide is an essential step in gaining better insight into the complex nature of cancer biology. This unprecedented insight will contribute towards optimizing cancer treatments and more accurately monitor disease response and progression. In addition the identification of novel biomarkers will allow the pairing of effective treatments in a timely manner for cancer patients. 奖励荣誉:Academic Awards:2017 : Speaker at the EMBO centrosome and spindle pole bodies conference EMBL Heidelberg Germany.2014 : British Society of Cell Biology (BSCB) Honor Fellow Travel Award UK.2009 : Prize winner of a paper presentation competition at the British Society for Cell Biology North England Cell Biology Forum University of York UK.2008 : Wellcome project grant University of Hull UK.2004 : “John A Beardmore Prize” for most outstanding performance in undergraduate medical genetics studies University of Wales Swansea UK.2004 : “John Hayward Prize” for being an overseas student who has shown the greatest benefit from studies in the School of Biological Sciences University of Wales Swansea UK.
